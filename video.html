The video's description

This video is a short trailer for the last DLC called Holy Fury. During the video, we can see a medieval helmet, that is halved verticaly. The left-hand side, there is a pagan brutes helmet, with white painting marks, black dirty iron, and a deers antler growing out of it. On the right-hand side, there is a helmet of a Teutonic knight, with polished shining iron, closed of mouth area, and a helmet decoration that resembles a wing. In the background there are flashes and sparks on a pitch black background. Meanwhile, the narrator says the following lines: "You too would believe in demons, if you'd seen what I have seen. Armies of frenzied warriors bellowing eerie hymns to strange Gods. Conversion of this monstrous horde seems impossible, for their ways are not our ways. But we must stop them here, the true God protects us!" During the whole speech, we can hear some menacing music.
