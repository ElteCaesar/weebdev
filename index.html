About the game

This game came out in 2012. Developed by Paradox Interactive (their official website), it aimed to simulate the world of medieval Europe, Asia, and Northern Africa. Powerful noble families rule different realms all over the map. They forge alliances, feast, and drink with each other. Also, they scheme, and wage war against their enemies. Through your decisions, you decide the fate of countless people. Lead your family to prosper, and rule, or to suffer and be neglected, it is all up to you. Furthermore, there are many historical figures and dynasties in the game. You can become William The Conqueror, and bend England to your knees. Take to the steppes of Eastern Europe and help the Árpád dynasty invade the Carpathian basin. Gather your most trusted soldiers from across Sweden and pillage The African coast as Björn Ironside. The list keeps on giving and giving, and there is simply no end to the interesting settings, from our history.
